Auditory Plugins

Plugins for in vivo electrophysiology on auditory neurons.

  • AuditoryNeuron: Spiking neuron model stimulated through an auditory transduction chain.
  • AuditorySearch: Periodically emits a search stimulus.
  • AuditorySession: Control recordings from auditory neurons.
  • AuditoryTraces: Variables for standard output traces of acoustic stimuli and recordings from microphones.
  • CalibSpeakers: Calibrating attenuation for loudspeakers.
  • FICurve: Optimized measuring of f-I curves.
  • FIField: Optimized measuring of f-I curves.
  • IsoResponse: Measures f-I-curves for a superposition of two sine waves.
  • LoudSpeaker: Direct conversion to attenuation level depending on carrier frequency.
  • OneClick: A single short click.
  • SingleStimulus: Output of a single stimulus stored in a file.
  • SpikePrecision: Assess spike precision in locust auditory receptors.
  • SysLatency: Measures latency of the whole system, i.e. signal delay, transduction, synapses, axonal delay, ...
