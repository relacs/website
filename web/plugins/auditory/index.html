Auditory Plugins

Plugins for in vivo electrophysiology on auditory neurons.

  • FICurve: Optimized measuring of f-I curves.
  • FIField: Optimized measuring of f-I curves.
  • ReceptorModel: Spiking neuron model stimulated through an auditory transduction chain.
  • Search: Periodically emits a search stimulus.
  • Session: Control recordings from auditory neurons.
  • SingleStimulus: Output of a single stimulus stored in a file.
  • SysLatency: Measures latency of the whole system, i.e. signal delay, transduction, synapses, axonal delay, ...
